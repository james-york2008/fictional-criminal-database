High Risk Threat Report:

Lyra Moonshard

Classification: HIGH-RISK
Subject ID: LM-0427
Status: Unknown Whereabouts

SUBJECT DESCRIPTION

Lyra Moonshard presents as a lean, pale figure with an unnerving, moonlit complexion overlaid by faint, shifting constellations that intensify during astral activity, marking her immediately as a rift-aligned threat. Her eyes—fractured, nebula-like irises with unstable pupils—show clear evidence of prolonged exposure to spatial distortion, while her hair, dark at the roots and fading to a drifting silver, behaves as though caught in perpetual low-gravity sway. She typically wears reinforced, travel-worn layers threaded with metallic filaments and fitted with unidentified crystalline components, all of which appear designed to stabilize or conceal her manipulation of astral energy. Field agents report a constant, low-grade distortion around her person, accompanied by motes of astral light, suggesting she is never fully anchored to this plane.

KNOWN ACTIVITIES

Lyra Moonshards documented activities across the Skyblock Archipelago indicate a consistent pattern of high-risk, high-impact operations centered around rift anomalies and astral flux zones. Surveillance logs place her at multiple pre-collapse sites mere hours before spatial destabilization events, suggesting either direct involvement or opportunistic exploitation of unfolding phenomena. She has been observed harvesting volatile rift-crystals from unstable territories, often dismantling local containment wards in the process, and has engaged in targeted infiltrations of research outposts to acquire restricted astral-mapping schematics. Witness reports describe her initiating “silent breaches”—localized spatial folds used to bypass security measures or relocate assets without trace—most recently during the unaccounted disappearance of three classified artifacts from the Aetherwatch Vault. Despite her destructive capabilities, she demonstrates selective restraint, at times providing tactical assistance to isolated settlements when such cooperation appears to benefit her long-term objectives. Notably, her continued presence near celestial convergence zones indicates preparation for a large-scale operation of unknown intent, with all current evidence supporting the assessment that Lyra Moonshards activities remain deliberate, escalating, and strategically aligned toward goals incompatible with public safety.

THREAT ASSESSMENT

Lyra Moonshard is assessed as a Level 5 Threat due to her unparalleled capacity to manipulate astral and rift-based energies, her demonstrated willingness to operate within populated or strategically sensitive zones, and her persistent evasion of containment efforts. Her ability to induce or accelerate spatial destabilization events presents a catastrophic risk, with projected casualties increasing exponentially should she choose to deploy these abilities with intent rather than incidental effect. Behavioral analysis indicates a highly intelligent, tactically adaptive subject who exhibits no predictable allegiance structure and demonstrates a pronounced capability for long-term planning, often using misleading patterns of cooperation to obscure her true objectives. Engagement protocols classify her as non-approachable under all field conditions; even indirect confrontation has resulted in personnel loss due to sudden environmental collapse or unanticipated astral surges. Given her expanding interest in celestial convergence points and her escalating procurement of high-grade arcane materials, current models forecast a significant probability that she is preparing a large-scale event with unknown but potentially existential implications. Extreme caution is not only advised—it remains mandatory for any operative assigned to zones in which Moonshard activity is suspected.

RECCOMENDED ACTIONS

  • Do not approach subject without full tactical support.
  • Monitor known rift zones for unusual activity.
  • Collect and report all sightings immediately to central command.
